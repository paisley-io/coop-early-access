Frequently asked questions

What is Paisley?

Paisley is a platform that lets freelancers get paid before they work. Instead of waiting for invoices, you can sell your future time as Time Credits and receive income upfront.

What are Time Credits?

Time Credits represent your future working hours. If you charge $75 per hour, you can mint hours in advance and sell them to clients who will redeem that time later.

Do I still have to do the work?

Yes. Time Credits are a commitment to deliver your work in the future. As clients redeem them, you complete the work and build your reputation.

Who is this for?

Freelancers and creators who sell their time: designers, developers, editors, coaches, consultants, and anyone working independently.

Why is this better than invoicing?

You get paid upfront instead of waiting 30 days. No chasing payments. No unpredictable cash flow.

What about platform fees?

Paisley is built as a user-owned network, not a traditional platform. The goal is to reduce fees and return more value to the people doing the work.

What happens if I do not deliver?

Your reputation is tied to delivery. Completing work builds trust and future demand. Failing to deliver reduces your ability to sell Time Credits.
